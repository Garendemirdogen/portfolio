About Me

My name is Garen Demirdogen. I live about 20 miles north of Boston, MA. Becoming a father has changed me in so many ways and I love everything about it. I love to do yard work, play ultimate frisbee and hang out with friends and family. I have always had a love and passion for anything tech related. I started working at the age of 16 as a lifeguard and camp counselor and developed a very good work ethic. I went on to run a production line at a local adhesive plant before being promoted to a Quality Control Technician for 3 years. From 2016 until 2024 I worked as an EMT and HVAC Mechanic. Currently I work as a web developer and project manager for a environmentally friendly lubricant company. I love learning new skills and becoming a "multi-tool" of knowledge.

I have an associates degree in criminal justice from North Shore Community College in Danvers, MA. My childhood dream was to become a police officer but as I grew older I realized I just love helping people. That is one of the main reasons I became an EMT. I am always ready to learn new things and love a challenge. I took a Full Stack Developer Bootcamp run by Columbia University and passed with a 90% in November 2023.
